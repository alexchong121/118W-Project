What we do...


Amazon Preservation Association works directly with indigenous people and all those who see the Amazon as their home. We strive to secure their land rights and prevent any further destruction. As technology advances, we look to train indigenous communities on this subject so it may help them with agriculture and the protection of their forests. By directing our efforts with indigenous communities, we join individuals who are profoundly inspired to uphold their genealogical lands with appropriate training, tools, and resources to ensure the preservation of the Amazon.

Join us as we aim to save the Amazon Rainforest.
